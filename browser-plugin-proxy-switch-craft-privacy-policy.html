Privacy Policy for Proxy Switch Craft

Effective Date: July 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Proxy Switch Craft is a browser extension designed to help users easily manage and switch proxy settings. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information Collection

Proxy Switch Craft does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All proxy profiles and most settings are stored locally in your browser and are not shared with any third party. However, proxy rules may use browser sync storage to synchronize across devices. These synchronized rules are not stored locally, but are managed by your browser's sync service and are not transmitted to any external server by this extension.

3. Permissions

  • Storage: Used to save your proxy profiles and extension settings locally.
  • Proxy: Used to manage and switch your browser's proxy settings.
  • WebRequest: Used to apply proxy rules to your browsing activity. No browsing data is collected or transmitted.

4. Data Security

All data managed by Proxy Switch Craft remains on your device. We do not access, collect, or transmit your browsing activity or proxy configurations.

5. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact
